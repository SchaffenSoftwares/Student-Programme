Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Schaffen Student Programme?

    The Schaffen Student Program,e is a global group of on-campus ambassadors who are eager to help students and their communities, lead in their local tech circles, and develop technical and career skills for the future. As a Student Ambassador, you might find yourself organizing a campus or community hackathon, mentoring other students.

  • Who is eligible to apply for the Programme and what experience do you need?

    To apply, you must be at least 16 years old, have valid identification and be actively enrolled in an accredited, education academic institution. We encourage students from all academic backgrounds who want to grow their career and tech skills while making a difference in their communities to apply. Some experience in coding or technology is helpful, but we have tools and resources to help you learn in the program.

  • What does it mean to be a School Ambassador?

    It means you’ll be supported while you build your local community, make a difference, lead others, learn and share. You’ll get goodies from our sponsors and experts to help you on your journey, including exclusive entry into our global student network.

  • What do I do to get these great benefits?

    As it turns out, earning these benefits involves doing some pretty fun things. We have four milestones for our Student Ambassadors:

    1. SPACE STONE - Promote our Products, Bring at least 30 Bloggers and Total of 100 Participants in our events.

    2. REALITY STONE - Bring 2 Ambassadors from 10 Different Schools, Make 200 Registrations in our Webinars and Promote our products.

    3. POWER STONE - Form your own tech community of at least 40 people and host an intra-school event powered by us.

    4. MIND STONE - Host an inter-school event powered by us and provide data or do some research work for our future products.

    5. TIME STONE - Still a Mystery!

  • How much time commitment is required?

    The program will give you back as much as you want to invest in it, and time commitment is flexible around your other responsibilities. The more time you invest in learning, leading, hosting workshops, and such, the more likely you are to achieve additional milestones and access new benefits.

  • Where can I get ideas for hosting an event or workshop?

    All Schaffen Student Ambassadors have access to our global student network and our experts on Discord Server, where the community posts updates on workshops and events they’ve hosted, tech skills they’re learning, opportunities to connect, and more.

  • How does the program help me in school or my career?

    The Student Ambassadors program prepares students for future careers through opportunities to build vibrant communities and share the latest in tech with their peers.
